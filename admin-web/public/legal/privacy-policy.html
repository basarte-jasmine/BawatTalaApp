Bawat Tala Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Bawat Tala ("we," "our," "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when you use our mobile app, admin web panel, and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account details such as name, email address, student number, program, and address fields.
  • Authentication and security data such as OTP verification status and hashed passwords.
  • Uploaded school ID images and extracted OCR text used for verification.
  • Usage metadata such as timestamps and operational logs required for security and support.
  • Google Calendar data only when an authorized admin explicitly grants calendar permission.

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user and admin accounts.
  • To verify identity and eligibility through school ID and email OTP checks.
  • To secure sign-in, password reset, and fraud prevention controls.
  • To provide platform features, analytics summaries, and appointment management.
  • To maintain, troubleshoot, and improve service quality.

3. Legal Basis and Consent

By using Bawat Tala and submitting your information, you consent to processing necessary to provide core platform functions, account security, and authorized admin operations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers strictly for service operation, including cloud database/authentication providers, email delivery providers, and Google APIs for authorized calendar functionality.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as required for active service usage, security, and legal or compliance obligations. Data may be deleted or anonymized when no longer needed.

6. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. Passwords are stored in hashed form. While we work to protect information, no method of transmission or storage is absolutely secure.

7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, or update of your personal information by contacting us.

8. Third-Party Services

Third-party services integrated with Bawat Tala (such as Google APIs) are governed by their own policies and terms. Please review them before granting permissions.

9. Children's Privacy

Bawat Tala is intended for users authorized under applicable institutional and legal requirements. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children in violation of applicable law.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates are effective when posted on this page.
